Spiritual growth isn't an event — it's a daily, disciplined act of faithfulness. Now it has a home.
"Walk by the Spirit, and you will not gratify the desires of the flesh." — Galatians 5:16
MyWalk is a faith-based habit-tracking app for Christians who want their daily disciplines to feel less like a to-do list — and more like an act of worship.
Built from the ground up around a Christian worldview, MyWalk frames every habit as an offering to God, every setback as grace, and every week as a fresh dedication. Whether you're building a prayer rhythm, practising Scripture reading, fasting, or breaking a difficult habit, MyWalk gives your walk structure, accountability, and community.
Daily Giving
Your habits aren't tasks — they're gifts. Each day you open your Give tab, check off your habits, and present them to God. A simple, beautiful act of offering.
Grace Over Streaks
MyWalk never punishes a missed day. Retroactive logging is supported up to six days back. Each Sunday begins a fresh dedication ceremony — new mercies, new commitment.
Fruit of the Spirit
Tag each habit with one or more of the nine Fruits of the Spirit (Galatians 5:22–23). Track how Love, Joy, Peace, Patience, and Self-Control are growing in your daily life.
Prayer Circles
Small, private accountability groups. Share gratitudes, post prayer requests, track shared habits, exchange encouragements, and check in weekly — without social media noise.
SOS Temptation Support
In a moment of temptation, press the SOS button. Your purpose statement and anchor verse appear instantly. Optionally alert trusted circle members to pray for you right now.
Your Spiritual Journey
Celebrate milestones — 30 days of prayer, 100 hours of Scripture, a year of fasting. A full Year in Review heatmap shows the beauty of your faithfulness over time.

About the App
Built for the serious disciple
Most habit apps are designed for productivity. MyWalk is designed for faithfulness. Every feature — the week cycle, the dedication ceremony, the prayer circles, the fruit portfolio — is built around a conviction: that spiritual growth is not a personal achievement, but a cooperative work with God and community.
How it Works
Each Sunday, you dedicate your week to God in a brief ceremony. Each day, you open the Give tab and check in your habits — Scripture reading, prayer, fasting, service, exercise, or whatever you're building. At week's end, you look back honestly before beginning again. Simple. Sustainable. Sacred.
Privacy First
Anonymous posting on gratitudes, prayer requests, and encouragements. Your data never fuels advertising. Your spiritual life is yours alone to share — on your terms, with people you choose.

Community
Prayer Circles — accountability without pressure
Join a small group of trusted believers. Share what you're grateful for each day. Post prayer requests. Encourage one another with a word or a "Praying for you today." Check in weekly with how you're really doing — Encouraged, Steady, Struggling, or Needs Prayer — and know that your circle will respond with grace.

Circles can also track shared habits together — a family fasting together, a small group memorising the same passage, friends holding each other to daily prayer.
